Reykjavík Radiance: Northern Lights & Nordic Nights

Duration: 6 Days / 5 Nights

Best For: Adventure Seekers | Winter Lovers | Photographers

Overview: Discover Reykjavík, the heart of Iceland’s natural wonders and vibrant culture. This curated winter adventure highlights the best of geothermal relaxation and aurora chasing.

  • 5-night stay in a centrally located boutique hotel
  • Guided Northern Lights tour
  • Blue Lagoon premium entry and spa experience
  • Golden Circle tour with Thingvellir, Geysir, and Gullfoss
  • Icelandic horse ride across lava fields
  • Daily breakfast and welcome dinner

Why You'll Love It: Reykjavík is a one-of-a-kind blend of untouched nature and warm, modern hospitality—perfect for a cozy winter wonderland escape.

Travel Season: October to March for best chance to see Northern Lights.
