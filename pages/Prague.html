Prague Delight: Bohemian Rhapsody

Duration: 6 Days / 5 Nights

Best For: History Buffs | Couples | Solo Travelers

Overview: Wander the cobblestone streets of Prague, where centuries-old architecture meets vibrant street life. This package reveals the magic of the Czech capital through its history, gastronomy, and romantic ambiance.

  • 5-night stay in a boutique hotel in Old Town
  • Welcome dinner at a traditional Czech tavern
  • Guided walking tour of Prague Castle, Charles Bridge, and Old Town Square
  • Vltava River cruise with drinks and live music
  • Day trip to Český Krumlov (UNESCO World Heritage Site)
  • Czech beer tasting and food pairing tour

Why You'll Love It: Prague’s fairy-tale scenery, deep cultural roots, and laid-back café life offer an elegant yet accessible European escape.

Travel Season: April to June and September to October for mild weather and fewer tourists.
