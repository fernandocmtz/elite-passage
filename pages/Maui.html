Maui Moments: Sea to Summit Experience

Duration: 7 Days / 6 Nights

Best For: Families | Couples | Nature Lovers

Overview: From volcanic craters to coral reefs, Maui offers unmatched natural diversity. This curated package highlights the best of Hawaii’s second-largest island with relaxation and thrill in equal parts.

  • 6-night stay in an oceanfront resort in Wailea or Lahaina
  • Full-day Road to Hana tour with waterfall stops
  • Snorkeling trip to Molokini Crater and Turtle Town
  • Authentic Hawaiian luau with live music and hula
  • Sunrise excursion to Haleakalā National Park with breakfast
  • Whale watching (seasonal: Dec–Apr)

Why You'll Love It: Perfect for nature lovers and beach seekers, Maui’s lush rainforests, vibrant marine life, and laid-back atmosphere make it a must-experience paradise.

Travel Season: April to November offers ideal weather and fewer crowds.
