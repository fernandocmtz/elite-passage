Bali Balance: Nature, Spirit & Soul

Duration: 10 Days / 9 Nights

Best For: Spiritual Seekers | Adventure Travelers | Wellness Enthusiasts

Overview: Explore Bali’s rich spiritual culture, awe-inspiring natural landscapes, and artisan traditions in this transformative journey through the Island of the Gods.

  • Stays in boutique hotels in Ubud, Canggu, and Seminyak
  • Morning yoga & wellness sessions
  • Guided tour of Tirta Empul Water Temple & Uluwatu Temple
  • Traditional Balinese cooking class with market visit
  • Monkey Forest, Tegallalang Rice Terraces, and Mount Batur sunrise hike
  • Free day in Nusa Dua for beaches or surfing

Why You'll Love It: This journey blends spiritual peace with bold adventure and rich local traditions, creating a life-changing cultural escape.

Travel Season: April to October (dry season).
