Bora Bora Bliss: Overwater Dream Getaway

Duration: 6 Days / 5 Nights

Best For: Honeymooners | Luxury Travelers | Couples

Overview: Nestled in French Polynesia’s Leeward Islands, Bora Bora is the epitome of tropical elegance. This luxury travel package offers a serene escape in overwater bungalows with breathtaking views of the turquoise lagoon and Mount Otemanu.

  • 5-night stay in a private overwater bungalow
  • Welcome flower lei & champagne
  • Daily gourmet breakfast & sunset beach dinners
  • Guided snorkeling tour with reef sharks and rays
  • Private motu (islet) picnic with local delicacies
  • Spa treatment for two
  • Roundtrip boat transfers from Bora Bora Airport

Why You'll Love It: Experience complete tranquility, impeccable service, and romantic touches that turn your trip into a postcard-perfect memory.

Travel Season: May to October for the driest and sunniest weather.
